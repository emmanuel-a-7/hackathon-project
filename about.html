About Our Mission

Why we built this tool, and how it helps the planet.

The Problem

The problem this website solves is the problem of unnecessary e-waste in the world. Creating phones and computers requires valuable materials and energy such as lithium, cobalt, and gold. Every year, over 50 million tonnes of electronic waste is generated globally — and much of it comes from devices that could have been repaired instead of thrown away.
