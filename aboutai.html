About Our Mission

Why we built this tool, and how it helps the planet.

🌍

The Problem

The problem this website solves is the problem of unnecessary e-waste in the world. Creating phones and computers requires valuable materials and energy such as lithium, cobalt, and gold. Every year, over 50 million tonnes of electronic waste is generated globally — and much of it comes from devices that could have been repaired instead of thrown away.

🔧

Our Solution

Making a website for users to diagnose their issue allows them to determine how complex their problem is, and see if they can choose to repair or recycle their device instead of throwing it away. Our severity scale gives you a clear, honest answer about whether a repair is worth it — or if responsible recycling is the better option.

♻️

How It Works

Select your device, choose your issue, and receive an instant diagnosis powered by a curated database of real-world Apple hardware problems. Each result includes a repairability score from 1 to 10, so you know exactly where you stand — from a simple at-home fix to a professional repair or responsible recycling.
